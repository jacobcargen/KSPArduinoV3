Simpit Features

  • 5 LCDs which provide flight information, like heading, roll, pitch, speed, altitude, waypoints, and 12 selectable views
  • 5 bar graphs showing essential resources, including fuel and power
  • 10 warning lights
    • GPWS and OWS, with voice warnings
    • Additional audio warnings are stall, gear, and brake
  • One rotation joystick and another joystick doubles as either translation or rotation
  • Trim control for both joysticks
  • Slide throttle
  • Adjustable precision control mode
  • Actions controls for major systems, like gear, flaps, stage, abort, and 10 more
  • Stability assist with 10 selectable modes and autopilot
  • Safety locks for mission-critical actions like stage and abort

Dependencies

  • Kerbal Space Program: 1.12.15
  • Supported Platform: Linux
  • KerbalSimpit Fork: jacobcargen/KerbalSimpitRevamped
  • KSP Mods: Alternate Resource Panel
